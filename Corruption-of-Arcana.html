About the game

What is Corruption of Arcana?


Corruption of Arcana is a Tarot themed card combat RPG where you must discover cards and weapons to fight against the corruption and the enemies that serve it. Quest in a 2.5D world and defend the people, learn their stories and discover new lands with unique environments

What's in the game?

Features and things about the game


Whilst the game may not look too big, there are quite a few things to do(and even more we plan to add)

When out exploring and saving the realm, you may feel like you've reached the end of your journey, when in fact; It has only just begun.


Even though some features may not be available until the main story has been complete, players are still able to do anything from the list below:


  • Defeat all of the hidden bosses*
  • Conquer the dungeons and reap the rewards*
  • Complete a range of side quests from most NPCs*
  • Collect all the Tarot cards and complete a full deck
  • Claim bounties from the bounty board in the main city*
  • Fill out Taros journal by finding secrets and defeating enemies
*not fully implemented into the current game state
